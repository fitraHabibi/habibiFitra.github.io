Perfume Storage Tips

Proper storage keeps your perfume’s fragrance fresh and long-lasting. Follow these simple tips to maintain its quality:

  • Keep Away from Heat: Avoid placing perfume near windows or in warm areas — high temperatures can alter the scent.
  • Store in the Original Box: The box protects the bottle from light exposure, helping preserve its composition.
  • Close the Cap Tightly: Prevent air from entering the bottle to slow down oxidation.
  • Avoid Humid Places: Don’t store perfumes in bathrooms; humidity can degrade the fragrance over time.
  • Use a Cool, Dark Spot: A drawer, cabinet, or vanity away from sunlight is ideal for storage.
