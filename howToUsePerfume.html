How to Use Perfume

Step 1: Where to Apply Perfume

Learn the key pulse points to apply your perfume for maximum effect: behind the ears, wrists, inner elbows, and collarbones.

Step 2: How Much Perfume to Use

Discover the right amount of spritz to avoid overpowering or fading too quickly. Tip: 2-3 sprays are ideal.

Step 3: Tips for Long-Lasting Fragrance

Boost your fragrance longevity with these tricks: moisturize your skin, avoid rubbing your wrists together, and layer your scents.
