Perfume Care

Step 1: How to Store Your Perfume

Keep your perfumes in a cool, dark place away from direct sunlight and heat. Avoid storing them in humid environments like bathrooms.

Step 2: Cleaning and Maintaining Bottles

Wipe your perfume bottles regularly with a soft cloth to maintain their shine and elegance.

Step 3: Signs Your Perfume is Expiring

Identify when a perfume is past its prime: discoloration, unusual smell, or evaporation.
